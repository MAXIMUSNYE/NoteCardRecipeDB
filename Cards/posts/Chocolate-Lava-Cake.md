## Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe

This recipe makes 4 servings.

### Ingredients
#### For the cake:
| Ingredient | Quantity |
|------------|----------|
| Semi-sweet chocolate | 4 oz |
| Unsalted butter | 1/2 cup |
| All-purpose flour | 1/4 cup |
| Powdered sugar | 1/2 cup |
| Egg | 2 |
| Egg yolk | 2 |
| Salt | 1/4 tsp |
 
#### For the lava filling:
| Ingredient | Quantity |
|------------|---------|
| Semi-sweet chocolate | 2 oz |
| Unsalted butter | 2 tbsp |
| Powdered sugar | 1/4 cup |
| Vanilla extract | 1/4 tsp |

### Steps
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Grease and flour 4 ramekins. Set aside.
3. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt 4 oz of semi-sweet chocolate and 1/2 cup of unsalted butter in 30-second intervals, stirring in between until melted and smooth.
4. In a separate bowl, whisk together 1/4 cup all-purpose flour and 1/2 cup powdered sugar.
5. Add 2 eggs, 2 egg yolks, and 1/4 tsp salt to the flour mixture. Whisk until smooth.
6. Pour the melted chocolate mixture into the flour mixture and whisk until combined.
7. Fill each ramekin halfway with the chocolate batter.
8. In another microwave-safe bowl, melt 2 oz of semi-sweet chocolate and 2 tbsp of unsalted butter in 30-second intervals, stirring in between until melted and smooth.
9. Add 1/4 cup of powdered sugar and 1/4 tsp of vanilla extract to the chocolate mixture. Stir until combined.
10. Divide the chocolate filling evenly among the 4 ramekins, spooning the filling on top of the cake batter.
11. Top off each ramekin with the remaining chocolate batter.
12. Place the ramekins on a baking sheet and bake for 14-16 minutes, or until the edges are firm and the center is soft.
13. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes.
14. Run a knife around the edges of each ramekin and invert onto a plate.
15. Serve warm with a scoop of ice cream or whipped cream. Enjoy!
