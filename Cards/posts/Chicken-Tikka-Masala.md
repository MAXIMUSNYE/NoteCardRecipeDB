# Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe

This classic Indian dish is a perfect blend of spices and tender chicken pieces slow-cooked in a rich tomato sauce. It's creamy and flavorful, and a perfect dinner choice for any occasion.

## Ingredients

### For Chicken Marinade:

|Ingredients | Quantity |
|---|---|
|Chicken breast, boneless | 1 pound|
|Plain yogurt | 1/2 cup |
|Ginger garlic paste | 2 tbsp |
|Turmeric powder | 1/2 tsp |
|Coriander powder | 1 tsp |
|Cumin powder | 1 tsp |
|Garam masala powder | 1 tsp |
|Salt | to taste |

### For Masala Sauce:

|Ingredients | Quantity |
|---|---|
|Butter | 2 tbsp |
|Onion, chopped | 1 large |
|Tomatoes, chopped | 3 medium |
|Green chilies, chopped | 2-3 |
|Ginger garlic paste | 1 tbsp |
|Turmeric powder | 1/2 tsp |
|Coriander powder | 1 tsp |
|Cumin powder | 1 tsp |
|Garam masala powder | 1 tsp |
|Red chili powder | 1/2 tsp |
|Heavy cream | 1/4 cup |
|Water | 1 cup |
|Salt | to taste |
|Cilantro leaves, chopped | for garnish |

## Instructions

1. Wash the chicken breast and pat it dry with a paper towel. Cut it into small bite-size pieces.

2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the marinade ingredients - 1/2 cup yogurt, 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste, 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp garam masala powder, and salt to taste.

3. Add the chicken pieces into the marinade mixture and stir well to coat the chicken thoroughly. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

4. Preheat the oven to 400°F and place the chicken pieces on a baking sheet. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until cooked through.

5. In a large skillet, melt 2 tbsp of butter over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until they are translucent.

6. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies, and continue cooking for another 5 minutes or until the tomatoes are soft.

7. Add 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste and stir well. Then add 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp garam masala powder, 1/2 tsp red chili powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.

8. Add 1 cup water and bring the mixture to a boil. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until the sauce thickens.

9. Turn off the heat and add heavy cream to the pan, stirring well.

10. Add the cooked chicken pieces and coat them with the sauce.

11. Garnish with chopped cilantro leaves and serve hot with rice or naan. Enjoy your delicious Chicken Tikka Masala!
